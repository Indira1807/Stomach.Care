Frequently Asked Questions

Stomach diseases encompass various conditions that affect the health of the stomach, such as gastritis, stomach ulcers, and acid reflux. These conditions can cause symptoms like stomach pain, nausea, and indigestion.

Stomach diseases can be prevented in several ways, such as:
  1. Avoiding spicy, fatty, and acidic foods
  2. Reducing alcohol and caffeine consumption
  3. Managing stress through relaxation techniques
  4. Not smoking

Many cases of stomach diseases can be treated and well-managed. With proper diagnosis and appropriate treatment, patients can experience significant improvement. However, some conditions may require long-term care.
